NAMSAD ARSHAQUE

A good place for dining but there is a lack of hygiene. We went here for a highly anticipated french breakfast but to our dismay they don’t serve that during the mornings. So instead we opted for some coffee and some cake instead. I really loved the soft and incredibly fluffy cakes which melted in your mouth with a slight crunch. I also loved the coffee. It was good and texture was silky and creamy. However, the hygiene was mediocre. The tables were dirty and crows were all over the place. Please improve on this. Overall, a good place for breakfast but only if you want South Indian food or cakes.
